## Working Papers

<div class="card paper">
  <div class="paper-title">Resolving the Higher-Moment Pricing Puzzle: Systematic and Idiosyncratic Risk Across Penny and Non-Penny Stocks</div>
  <div class="paper-meta">with <strong>Mustafa Caglayan</strong> and <strong>Diogo Duarte</strong></div>
  <p>
    We examine whether variance, skewness, and kurtosis predict expected returns once decomposed into systematic and idiosyncratic components. Using U.S. common stocks from 1963–2024, we show that the mixed evidence in the literature reflects pooling two distinct markets.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="card paper">
  <div class="paper-title">Who Seeks Skewness? Payoff Engineering in Hedge Funds</div>
  <p>
    We examine whether hedge funds deliberately seek positively skewed, lottery-like payoffs and assess the economic implications of such behavior using a comprehensive panel from 1994 to 2024.
  </p>
</div>
